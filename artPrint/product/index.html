Little Boy on the Beach

This adorable Boy on the beach art print of a bright, young boy enjoying some time on the beach is a fantastic backdrop for guest signing or a baby shower. The adorable picture will add a bit of charm and cheer for any child's room or gift. The name on the poster can be altered to your needs for a boy.

01.

Make the first move

Text, call or email us to set up an appointment. Please leave your name, best way to get in touch as well as the best time to reach you.

___

02.

Wait for the call back

Once we receive your message, Young will return to you as soon as possible, within 24 to 48 hours. If you have a design idea, you can share that at this time. This is a time where you and Young discuss design concepts. Once you wish to proceed and Young agrees to take the job, you will need to make a one time non-refundable deposit of $15.00* to start the design concepts. Young will provide you, upon discussion of your time table, the design within the time agreed. Each design will be custom tailored to you.

___

03.

We have a match

You will receive a design proof sent to you by text, email or secured site that only you can view to see if the design is to your liking. If not, Young will go back to draft a new design until we get it right with no additional cost to you.
Once you pick a design, it could be revised until everything is 100% to your satisfaction. Once the final design is approved, we are on our way to place your order.

___

*$15.00 non-refundable deposit toward the final cost with unlimited revisions.
IN the event the client cancels without ordering, we keep $15.00.
